H1B Sponsor Checker Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-01-13

Overview

H1B Sponsor Checker is a browser extension that checks whether a company has H-1B sponsorship records and shows related roles. All lookups are performed locally in the extension.

Data Collection

The extension does not collect, transmit, or sell any personal data. It does not collect browsing history, page contents, or identifiers.

Data Storage

The extension stores a local copy of the H-1B index in Chrome storage to speed up lookups. This data is stored on your device only.

Third-Party Sharing

No data is shared with third parties.

Detailed description
H1B Sponsor Checker helps you quickly verify if a company filed H1B LCAs this year.

Features:
- Works on any page by detecting the company name.
- Shows H1B sponsorship status for the current year.
- Lists top roles associated with recent filings.
- Uses U.S. Department of Labor LCA disclosure data.
- Runs fully client-side with offline-ready caching.

Notes:
- Data reflects DOL LCA disclosure filings (not final USCIS approvals).
